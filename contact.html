❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer nut-free options?

Yes, we have a dedicated nut-free line, although some products are made in the same facility with strict protocols.

Can I order a custom cake with 24h notice?

We typically require 48-72h for custom orders to ensure the safest ingredients are sourced.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within the UK. We are working on EU shipping soon!

How should I store my treats?

Most bakes stay fresh for 3 days in an airtight container at room temperature.
