❓ Service FAQ

What is the lead time for custom cakes?

We require at least 14 days notice for wedding cakes and 7 days for specialty birthday cakes.

Do you deliver event orders?

Yes, we provide safe transport and setup for all large event orders within a 50-mile radius.

Can you match exact color themes?

We use natural, plant-based food coloring. While we can get very close, some neon shades may differ slightly.

Is there a minimum for wholesale?

Yes, we typically require a minimum weekly order of 24 items for wholesale partners.
