Baking with Purpose

Because health and happiness should go hand in hand.

Our Story

SafeBakes was born out of a simple need: a parent's desire to give their child a birthday cake that wouldn't make them sick. What started in a small home kitchen with gluten-free flour and vegan butter has grown into a community staple for those with dietary restrictions.

We don't just bake; we care. Every batch is tested, every recipe is refined until it's perfect, ensuring that "allergy-friendly" never means "boring."

Beyond the Basics

The SafeBakes Standard

While others follow rules, we set the standard. Our commitment to your health is backed by rigorous protocols and a dedicated allergen-free environment.

Daily Lab Testing

Each batch of incoming ingredients undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our zero-allergen threshold.

Isolated Production

Our kitchen is 100% dedicated. Gluten, dairy, and nuts are strictly prohibited from entering the facility.

Master Craftsmen

Our bakers are specialists in alternative flours and plant-based chemistry, ensuring taste is never compromised.

Our Foundation

The SafeBakes Promise

We believe that dietary restrictions should never mean restricting your joy. Our core values reflect our commitment to safety, honesty, and community.

Pure Integrity

We are 100% transparent about our sourcing. Every flour, sweetener, and binder is rigorously vetted for quality and safety.

Gold-Standard Safety

Zero cross-contamination isn't just a goal—it's our absolute mandate. We maintain high-level allergen protocols in every batch.

Inclusive Community

We bake for the child who's never had a birthday cake and the adult who misses home. Everyone belongs at SafeBakes.
