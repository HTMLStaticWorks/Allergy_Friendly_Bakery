Our Story

SafeBakes was born out of a simple need: a parent's desire to give their child a birthday cake that wouldn't make them sick. What started in a small home kitchen with gluten-free flour and vegan butter has grown into a community staple for those with dietary restrictions.

We don't just bake; we care. Every batch is tested, every recipe is refined until it's perfect, ensuring that "allergy-friendly" never means "boring."

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, delicious, and safe bakery products for everyone, regardless of their dietary needs or allergies, fostering an inclusive food culture.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted name in allergy-friendly baking, setting the gold standard for safety, transparency, and flavor.

💎 Our Core Values

Integrity

We are transparent about every ingredient we use.

Safety

Community

Baking treats that bring everyone together.
